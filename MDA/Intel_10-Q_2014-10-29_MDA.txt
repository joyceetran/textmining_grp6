Of the $10.0 billion of marketable debt instruments measured and recorded at fair value on a recurring basis and classified as Level 2, approximately 50% was classified as Level 2 due to the use of a discounted cash flow model, and approximately 50% was classified as such due to the use of non-binding market consensus prices that were corroborated with observable market data.
