Through our e-commerce business model, we offer visually inspired browsing, compelling merchandising, easy product discovery and attractive prices for over thirty-three million products from over 23,000 suppliers. 19 Table of Contents We believe an increasing portion of the dollars spent on home goods will be spent online and that there is an opportunity for acquiring more market share. We turn these customers into recurring shoppers by creating a seamless shopping experience across their entire journey offering best-in-class product discovery, purchasing, fulfillment and customer service.
