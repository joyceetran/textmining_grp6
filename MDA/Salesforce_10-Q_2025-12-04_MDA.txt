On July 4, 2025, the One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA) was enacted into law. The OBBBA includes significant changes to US corporate tax provisions of the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act. Notably, it allows an immediate deduction for domestic research and development expenditures, reinstates 100% bonus depreciation, and modifies the international tax framework. The changes had an immaterial impact to the Companys tax provision for the period ended October 31, 2025.
