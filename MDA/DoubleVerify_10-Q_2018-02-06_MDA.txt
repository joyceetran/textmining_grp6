DeVry University is the legacy business of Adtalem and at one time accounted for the majority of its consolidated revenue and operating income. Disposal of this operating segment will have a significant effect on the operations and financial results of Adtalem. DeVry University employs approximately 1,100 full-time faculty and staff and requires significant home office administrative support, absorbing approximately 30% of all home office administrative costs. 38 In accordance with U.S. Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP), we are classifying the DeVry University entity as Held for Sale and Discontinued Operations as of December 31, 2017.
